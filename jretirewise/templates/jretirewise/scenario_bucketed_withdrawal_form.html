About Bucketed Withdrawal

The bucketed withdrawal strategy allows you to define different withdrawal rates for different periods of retirement. For example:

  • Ages 55-59: Withdraw 2% (conservative pre-retirement buffer)
  • Ages 59-65: Withdraw 4.5% (all retirement accounts accessible)
  • Ages 65+: Withdraw 3% (Medicare reduces healthcare costs, Social Security kicks in)

After creating this scenario, you'll define specific withdrawal buckets and run the calculation to see your projected portfolio values.
